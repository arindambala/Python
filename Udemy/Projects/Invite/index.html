Recipe | Cinnamon Rolls

// Ingredients //

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

// Instructions //

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 mins.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hrs.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3 cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Preheat the oven to 350°F or 180°C, then bake the rolls for 20-25 mins until lightly brown.
